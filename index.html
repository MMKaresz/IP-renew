IP-renew

Simple IP renew for XP, Vista, Win7 (possible Win8, 8.1, 10 - not tested yet)

Simple script, to disconnect and reconnect to LAN / WLAN. Use careful with network drives! *This script written in AutoIt, some virus scanners can find harmful - because many viruses written in AutoIt.
